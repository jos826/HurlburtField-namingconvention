Welcome to the Hurlburt Field AFB Naming Convention website!
To use this website, follow these steps:

  • Choose the computer type (Workstation, Laptop, or Tablet) from the dropdown.
  • Select the squadron from the provided options.
  • Enter the serial number in the designated input field.
  • Choose the Manufacturer/Model from the dropdown.
  • Click on the "Generate Table" button to see the output in the table.
  • The "Name:" section below the table will display the generated name based on the input.

For any assistance or requests for changes, contact SrA Placencia, Jose at 884-9722.

The name is split up into 4 parts:

  • Identifier: For Hurlburt Field, this is FTEV
  • Computer Type: W, L, or T for workstation, laptop, or tablet
  • Squadron: An abbreviated version of the squadron the computer belongs to
  • Serial: The remaining spaces are filled with the beginning or end of the serial, based on what side is unique
